1.将 FightingVision2020.service 复制到 /ect/systemd/system/
2.将 FightingVision2020.sh 复制到 /bin/
3.修改 FightingVision2020.sh 中的程序绝对路径
3.sudo chmod +x /bin/FightingVision2020.sh
4.重新加载配置文件 sudo systemctl daemon-reload
5.添加开机自启 systemctl enable FightingVision2020.service
6.sudo reboot

Status:
systemctl status FightingVision2020.service （服务详细信息）
systemctl is-active FightingVision2020.service (仅显示是否 Active)
